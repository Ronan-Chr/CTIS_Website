About Us

We are a aociety of students a Centenary University who are passionate and dedicated to learning and innovation. Our mission is to continue developing our skills in programming and technology, while providing a collaborative environment for our members to learn and grow together. The Tech and Innovation Society (TIS) networking opportunities, workshops, guest speakers, and collaborative projects respresents how we are building a strong community of tech enthusiasts, preparing for careers in the tech industry, and fostering a culture of continuous learning and innovation.
